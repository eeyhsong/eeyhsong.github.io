Biography

Yonghao Song is currently a third-year master student in Shien-Ming Wu School of Intelligent Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2021, supervised by Prof. Longhan Xie. He is also a visiting student in Center of Human-Computer Interaction from Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, CAS, surpvised by Prof. Qingqing Zheng. Before that, he received the B. Eng. degree in School of Electrtronic and Information Engineering from SCUT in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Changxing Ding.

His research interests cover brain-computer interface (BCI) and deep learning, with emphasis on sensorimotor rhythm. He is dedicated to designing deep learning algorithms to better percieive key features from EEG signals (e.g. feature enhancement, classification). He is also interested in using adversarial learning to handle cross-subject problem, and hope to promote the practicality of BCI systems in rehabilitation.

He is now looking for a Ph.D. position in brain signal analysis.
